What is MNT Staking?

Mantle Rewards Station is Mantle's official rewards app for locking MNT, allocating Mantle Power to pools, and claiming available rewards. Mantle's tokenomics docs describe MNT as both a governance token and a utility token used for gas fees on Mantle Network and as a principal asset within Rewards Station.

How MNT Staking works

  1. Open the appGo to the official Mantle Rewards Station domain and connect the wallet that holds MNT.
  2. Lock MNTEnter the amount, review the lock terms, and confirm the transaction in your wallet.
  3. Choose durationSelect the lock-in period shown by the app; longer periods may affect Mantle Power.
  4. Allocate and claimAllocate Mantle Power to available reward pools, then return to the dashboard to view and claim rewards when available.

Before you stake

Official routing

The primary action should send users to Mantle Rewards Station, not to a clone, exchange, or unaffiliated staking page.

Locking, not validator staking

The app describes participation as locking MNT, building Mantle Power, allocating to reward pools, and claiming available rewards; no fixed APY should be assumed.

Mantle-native context

MNT is used across Mantle for governance and network utility, with token details published in Mantle's official docs.

MNT Staking FAQ

What is mnt staking?

It commonly refers to using Mantle Rewards Station to lock MNT, receive Mantle Power, allocate to reward pools, and claim available rewards through the official app.

Where should I do mnt staking safely?

Use the official app at rewards.mantle.xyz and verify the domain before connecting a wallet or signing any transaction.

Does mnt staking have a fixed APY?

Do not assume a fixed APY unless it is displayed by the official app for a specific pool or campaign; reward terms can vary by event and allocation.

Is mnt staking the same as network validator staking?

No. Mantle Rewards Station frames the flow as locking MNT and allocating Mantle Power to reward opportunities, not running or delegating to validators.

Which network do I need for mnt staking?

Use the network and wallet prompts shown inside the official Mantle Rewards Station app, and keep enough gas available for required transactions.
